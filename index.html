Event Details ECHOR 2022

Echor consists of a variety of 9 events including: The Best Manager, The Best Management Team, Crisis Management, HR Game, Hackathon, Treasure Hunt, Speaking Event, The Startup Director and Photography. These events vary in genres, which enables students from all academics to have an equal say at winning.

Jain University Kochi, welcome students from the entirety of the state to showcase their talents and put those talents to test. We have events ranging from The Best Manager to Treasure Hunt and other activities to keep the the energy up. So, welcome to Echor!

Event Details

Everest - Best Manager

The Best Manager competition will be held to test the soft and hard skills to the test to bring out the best. Everest is the tallest mountain in the world and to be the Best Manager, you need to climb that peak. And on your way, you need an eye to carefully plan, a mind to scrutinize every alternative and a vision to reach the peak, to be the Best Manager..


Broad Peak - Crisis Management

Do you have what it takes to face a real, hardcore crisis? Do you have the agility to complete tasks on time? But, are you also ready to have some fun? Well, Broad Peak is here! A fun Crisis Management game that will test your confidence-filled strengths to the max. This four-member teamwork game will test your communication skills, time management and resources allocation skills.

Nuptse - Open Sprint

A fun, fast-paced, two-day collaborative event to hack and build projects for a healthy Internet and to protect the web we all love. It will be a diverse network of scientists, educators, artists, engineers, students and others as we come together in person and online to innovate in the open.


Annapurna - HR Game

Human beings are an inevitable part of this world, society and above all, work culture. This team-based competition will involve thinking proactively about the current HR scenarios, to be able to agree and disagree about an HR's role in a company and society and finally, to tickle that creative side of an HR. This two-day event will be interactive, communicative and exciting.

Kamet - Treasure Hunt

We need investigators from all over Kerala to solve conventional treasure hunt codes. Codes will be related to basic science, so breaking the code, thinking out of the box and finding the next clue all before the other teams find the treasure is what Kamet is about. So, bring your thinking mode on and break the almost-impossible scientific codes!







Shispare - Extempore

Is speaking within a time restraint your USP? Well, Echor's Shispare is right around the corner. On the spot, you will be given a topic dealing with a bizzare or revolutionary theory related to science and your goal is to convince the judges with your view. Fail to match with your opponents, you lose. So, if you have what it takes to stand from the rest, welcome!






Denali - Best Management Team

Are you a leader that can direct a team? Are you a great co-worker? Are a good handler of situations? If you fall under any of these three, Echor's Denali will open its doors for you. Climb the three rounds of Denali and you can redeem your status as The Best Management Team. So, call in your teammates and start coordinating!

Elbrus - Photography

The composure, the composition, the overall beauty of a picture speaks a lot. If you have an eye for clicking pictures of momentous occasions, Echor's Elbrus is waiting for you. This two round competition will aid you to look deeper than what you see on the outside. And make sure to bring your own capturing machine! So, click away!


Trivor - Startup Director

How's your finance-related knowledge? How's fluently do you speak in a minute? Do you have a startup idea that could be revolutionary? Echor's Trivor will offer you a chance to showcase all these talents. This three-round competition could change your life! So, what're you waiting for? Call in your mates and win big!

About Hiraeth

Hiraeth is a cultural spectacle that will be held on the second and final day of Echor. It will be a memorable evening filled with music, dance and all that good stuff! Our students will start off to dance to rhythms and sing to tunes. Then, you would sway to a music band that will tingle your ears with laid-back songs and then bang your heads to an explosive DJ set to finish Echor with a proper bang!
